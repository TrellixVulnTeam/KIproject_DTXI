Binary search:

mid = (min+max)/2

List a, where we will search for a number.

Let's suppose that we want to find number 57.

The lower and upper bounds are min and max.

Calculate mid(index of number which will be compared to 57)

mid = (min+max)/2

mid = (0 + 9)/2 => mid=4

a[mid] is compared with 57

As 11 is less than 57,

lower bound min is pointed to a[5]

Again mid is calculated

mid = (min + max) / 2

mid = (5 + 9) / 2 => mid = 7

a[mid] is compared with 57

As 25 is less than 57

lower bound min is pointed to a[8]

Again mid will be calculated => mid = 8

a[mid] is compared with 57

Success!
