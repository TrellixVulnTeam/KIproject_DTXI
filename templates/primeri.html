Task 1:

Switch the liquids from the two glasses

You can use an extra glass!

1. Take a new Glass (Glass 3)

2. Put the liquid from Glass 1 to Glass 3

3. Put the liquid from Glass 2 to Glass 1

4. Put the liquid from Glass 3 to Glass 2

This Sequence of Steps in which we get to a solution for a given task is called an Algorithm
